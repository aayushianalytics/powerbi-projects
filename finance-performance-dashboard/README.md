# Finance-performance-dashboard | Power BI


## 📌 Project Overview
Sales teams generate a large amount of performance data every month, but without proper reporting it becomes difficult to track progress against targets and understand overall performance clearly. This Power BI dashboard was built to simplify that process by bringing actual sales, target metrics, and team performance into one interactive and easy-to-analyze view.


## 🛠 Tech Stack
**Power BI** - Used to build the dashboard and create interactive visualizations for performance analysis.
**Power Query** - Used for data cleaning and transforming raw sales data into an analysis-ready format.
**DAX** - Used to create calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic business metrics.
**Microsoft Excel** - Used as the data source for sales, target and team performance data.


## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes monthly actual sales data, target sales data, salesperson-level performance details, including team information and sales trends used for KPI tracking and comparative analysis.


## 📋 Features
### ⚠️ Business Problem
Trackinig sales performance manually across multiple people and teams can get messy very quickly. It becomes difficult to understand who is meeting targets, where performance is dropping and which areas need attention.

### 🎯 Goal of Dashboard
The goal was to build a simple and interactive dashboard that gives a complete view of sales performance in one place and helps compare actual results with targets more efficiently.

### 📊 Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards (Top Section)** Displays overall actual sales, target sales, and performance variance to provide a quick snapshot of business performance.
- **Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)** Compares actual sales against target sales across different months to identify performance trends and fluctuations over time.
- **Team-wise Performance Breakdown (Bar Charts)** Highlights the contribution of different sales teams, making it easier to compare overall team performance.
- **Salesperson Performance Breakdown (Bar Chart)** Shows individual salesperson performance and helps identify top performers as well as underperforming representatives.
- **Performance Variance Analysis (Comparison Visuals)** Helps track the gap between actual and target values to understand where targets are consistently missed or exceeded.
- **Interactive Filters & Slicers** Allows users to filter the dashboard dynamically by team, salesperson, or time period for more focused analysis.

### 📈 Business Impact and Insights
- Helped highlight salesperson consistently performing above target
- Made it easier to identify underperforming months and teams
- Reduced the need for manual reporting by bringing everything into one dashboard
- Improved visibility into sales performance and overall business trends
