# Chocolate-Sales-Performance-Dashboard | Power BI


## 📌 Project Overview
Businesses generate large amounts of sales data every day, but without proper reporting it becomes difficult to track revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance effectively. This Power BI dashboard was built to bring all major sales insights into one place and make business performance easier to monitor and analyze.


## 🛠 Tech Stack
**Power BI** – Used to build the dashboard and create interactive visualizations for sales analysis.
**Power Query** – Used for cleaning, transforming, and preparing raw sales data for reporting.
**DAX** – Used to create calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic sales metrics.
**Microsoft Excel** – Used as the primary data source containing sales transaction and customer-related data.


## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes sales transactions, customer information, product categories, order details, and revenue-related data used for sales and performance analysis.


## 📋 Features
### ⚠️ Business Problem
Tracking sales performance manually across products, customers, and regions can become time-consuming and difficult to manage. Businesses often struggle to identify top-performing products, revenue trends, and areas where sales performance is declining.

### 🎯 Goal of Dashboard
The goal was to build an interactive dashboard that provides a complete overview of sales performance, helping users track revenue trends, monitor KPIs, and analyze customer and product behavior more efficiently.

### 📊 Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards (Top Section)** Displays overall sales, profit, orders, and other key business metrics to provide a quick overview of performance.
- **Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)** Shows revenue trends across different time periods to identify sales growth and seasonal fluctuations.
- **Category-wise Sales Breakdown (Bar Chart)** Highlights the performance of different product categories and helps identify high-revenue segments.
- **Regional Performance Analysis (Charts)** Compares sales performance across different regions to identify strong and weak markets.
- **Customer & Product Insights (Visuals)** Helps analyze customer purchasing patterns and top-performing products.
- **Interactive Filters & Slicers** Allows users to filter the dashboard dynamically by region, category, and time period for deeper analysis.

### 📈 Business Impact and Insights
- Helped identify top-performing product categories and regions
- Improved visibility into sales and revenue trends over time
- Made it easier to track business KPIs in one centralized dashboard
- Reduced manual reporting efforts through interactive visual analysis
- Enabled quicker decision-making using real-time sales insights
