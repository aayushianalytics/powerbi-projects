# Healthcare-Waitlist-Analysis-Dashboard | Power BI


## 📌 Project Overview
Healthcare systems handle large volumes of inpatient (IP) and outpatient (OP) cases every year, making it difficult to monitor waitlists, patient distribution, and treatment demand efficiently. This Power BI dashboard was built to analyze healthcare waitlist data across multiple years and provide a clearer view of patient trends, specialty demand, and overall waitlist performance.


## 🛠 Tech Stack
**Power BI** – Used to build the dashboard and create interactive visualizations for healthcare waitlist analysis.
**Power Query** – Used for cleaning, merging, and transforming multi-year inpatient and outpatient datasets.
**DAX** – Used to create calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic healthcare performance metrics.
**Microsoft Excel / CSV Files** – Used as the primary data source containing inpatient, outpatient, and specialty mapping data.


## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes multi-year inpatient (IP) and outpatient (OP) waitlist records along with specialty mapping data used to analyze patient demand, treatment categories, and healthcare waitlist trends.


## 📋 Features
### ⚠️ Business Problem
Managing healthcare waitlists manually across multiple specialties and patient categories can become difficult and time-consuming. Without centralized reporting, it is hard to identify rising patient demand, long waiting periods, and heavily burdened specialties.

### 🎯 Goal of Dashboard
The goal was to build an interactive dashboard that provides a complete overview of inpatient and outpatient waitlists, helping users monitor patient demand, analyze specialty-level trends, and identify areas requiring operational attention.

### 📊 Key Visuals
- **KPI Cards (Top Section)** Displays overall inpatient and outpatient waitlist counts along with yearly patient trends to provide a quick overview of healthcare demand.
- **Waitlist Trend Analysis (Line Chart)** Shows how inpatient and outpatient waitlists changed over multiple years to identify growth patterns and peak demand periods.
- **Specialty-wise Waitlist Breakdown (Bar Chart)** Highlights specialties with the highest patient waitlists, helping identify departments under the most pressure.
- **Patient Category Analysis (Comparison Visuals)** Compares inpatient and outpatient distributions to better understand treatment demand across categories.
- **Year-wise Waitlist Distribution (Charts)** Displays changes in patient waitlists across different years for trend comparison and performance tracking.
- **Interactive Filters & Slicers** Allows users to filter data dynamically by year, specialty, and patient category for more focused healthcare analysis.

### 📈 Business Impact and Insights
- Helped identify specialties with consistently high patient waitlists
- Made it easier to analyze long-term healthcare demand trends
- Improved visibility into inpatient and outpatient distribution patterns
- Reduced manual reporting efforts by centralizing multi-year healthcare data into one dashboard
- Enabled quicker analysis of patient load and operational bottlenecks
